Game Instructions

Player 1: Starts the game by choosing a secret number within a specified range.

Player 2: Attempts to guess the secret number chosen by Player 1. Has a total of five chances to guess correctly.

Player 3: If Player 2 fails to guess the number within five attempts, Player 3 gets a chance to guess. If Player 3 also fails to guess the number, then Player 1 wins the game.

Winning Conditions: If Player 2 guesses the number correctly within their five attempts, Player 2 wins. If Player 2 fails and Player 3 guesses correctly, Player 3 wins. If neither Player 2 nor Player 3 can guess the number, Player 1 wins.

This dynamic guessing game not only provides an engaging way to pass the time but also encourages friendly competition and sharpens your guessing abilities.
